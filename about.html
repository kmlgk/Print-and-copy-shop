Our story

From one copy counter to a full print studio

A dozen years, four expansions, and thousands of print runs later — here's how PaperCraft got here.

2014

A single copy counter

PaperCraft opened as a 400 sq ft storefront with one black & white copier and a promise: no job too small, no customer kept waiting.

2017

Wide-format & bindery added

We invested in our first large-format press and binding equipment, letting us take on banners, signage and spiral-bound reports in-house.

2021

Second location & design studio

A growing roster of small-business clients led us to open a second shop and hire our first in-house graphic designers for logo and layout work.

2026

Online ordering & live tracking

Today, customers upload files, get instant quotes and track jobs from any device — while our six shops keep the same hands-on care we started with.

Mission & values

What guides every job we run

Our mission is simple: make professional printing fast, affordable and painless for every business and individual we serve.

Speed & Reliability

Most jobs are ready within 24 hours, and our live status tracker means you always know exactly where your order stands.

Print Quality

Color-calibrated presses and a manual quality check on every order before it leaves the shop — no smudges, no misaligned cuts.

Sustainability

FSC-certified paper stock, soy-based inks, and a recycling program that reclaims offcuts from every large-format job.

Customer-First Support

Real people answer the phone. If a proof isn't right, we reprint it — no arguments, no restocking fees.
