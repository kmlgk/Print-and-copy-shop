Overview

Your first impression, printed right

A business card is often the only physical trace of your brand a new contact walks away with — so PaperCraft prints every card on heavyweight 14pt, 16pt or 32pt triple-layer stock, color-calibrated so what you see on screen is what lands in your hand. Upload a print-ready file or start from one of our free layout templates, and our press team will proof it before a single sheet is cut.

Choose from five finishes to match the feel you want a contact to remember: silky matte for a modern, low-glare look, high-gloss for bold color pop, natural uncoated for a tactile letterpress feel, luxury soft-touch for a velvety edge, or spot UV to raise your logo off the page with a glossy accent on a matte base. Rounded corners, square-cut and painted edges are available on every finish at no extra charge.

Orders of 250 or more ship with a dedicated proof review, and our same-day rush line means a reorder placed before 11am can be ready for pickup by closing. Every job — from a single 100-card starter box to a 1,000-card office restock — runs through the same quality check before it reaches your dashboard as "Ready for pickup."

FAQ

Business card printing, answered

Matte gives a smooth, low-glare surface that's easy to write on; gloss adds shine and makes colors pop for photo-heavy designs; soft-touch has a velvety, slightly rubberized coating that feels premium in hand. Spot UV layers a glossy raised accent over a matte base to highlight a logo or detail.
Yes. Every order includes access to our free business card templates in your dashboard — just swap in your logo, name and contact details. Need a custom layout instead? Our design add-on starts at $25 and includes two rounds of revisions.
Boxes start at 100 cards. Per-card pricing drops automatically at 250, 500 and 1,000 — the pricing table above shows the exact per-unit and total cost at each tier.
Rounded corners, square-cut and painted edges are available on every finish at no extra charge — just select the option when you upload your file. Die-cut custom shapes are available as a premium add-on; contact us for a quote.
